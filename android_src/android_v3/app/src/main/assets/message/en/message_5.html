Dr. Xu : the Technological Leader of Nano-crystal chip in the world

Dr. Xu

in 1986, graduated from China University of Science and Technology, then study abroad in French;

In 1988, get master degree of in polymer materials Industrial Physics and Chemistry High School in Paris, French;

In 1991, get the doctor degree in French National Center for Science

Dr. Xu, as the chief scientist and doctoral supervisor formerly in State University of New York at Albany Nano/MEMS system technology (MEMS/Nanosystems).

Dr. Xu, successfully led the integrate project of the processing line construction from University of Albany, with a total investment of more than 100 million dollars Silicon chip of 200 mm development. This project is the first processing platform for dedicated for research and development of MEMS devices in the field of 200 mm integrated nano/micro, and help create Institution of Nano Science and Engineering from State University of New York focused on the development and research of nano-tech in USA. It took a total investment of 5 billion dollars for the institution, and the first doctor of nano science was awarded in 2004.

Dr. Xu, as a member from nano institution committee, professor recruitment committee, post-doctor recruitment committee, nano science and engineering curriculum committee, degree qualification examination of nano science and nano engineering committee.
